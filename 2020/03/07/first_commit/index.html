Know thyself

After learning various programming languages, data structures and algorithms, and game engines, I tried to make a game. However, I found that I was at a loss about the familiar game engine, the familiar buttons, the familiar interface, but I couldn’t start at all.I suddenly realized that what I needed most was not programming skills, but creativity and the ability to control the big picture. My job was to be a designer, then a programmer.
